Pitch Video
Manroop Kalsi,

Co-founder, Toronto, Canada

Everyone deserves an equal chance at the gift of family, and the gift of reproduction. Those who face infertility struggle with immense burdens, and undergo a lot of physical and mental pain. It’s just not fair. One of the happiest moments in life is replaced with worry, struggle and fear. This is why we need Janam Labs.

Richa Pandya

Co-founder, Toronto, Canada

Current ARTs are extremely dependent on there being a high quantity and quality of eggs and sperm, and for many people facing infertility, that just isn’t possible. At Janam Labs, we’re reinventing reproduction by completely bypassing this need by turning somatic cells into healthy eggs and sperm.

Alisha Arora

Co-founder, Toronto, Canada

Janam Labs gives me hope for the future, with infertility rates increasing it is directly effecting emotional wellbeing. It is the answer to the mental well-being infertility crisis. I hope to see a world where we’re providing each family with the happiness of a lifetime.

Simran Mayra

Co-founder, Toronto, Canada

At Janam Labs we have 3 goals: decreasing cost, decreasing pain, and increasing effectiveness of fertility treatment. Say bye to painful hormone injections, and ovulation induction because we only need parent skin cells to produce a healthy baby for heterosexual and same sex couples.
