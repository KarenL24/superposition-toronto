Reinventing Reproduction

Giving everyone an equal chance at producing the gift of life.

Learn More
Our Mission

Building a future where reproduction is a worry of the past.

The Problem

n the past 40 years, the male sperm count has declined by 59%, it is projected that if this trend continues by 2045, the median sperm count will be 0. Already 1/8 couples are facing infertility, with this number rising as time goes on. Current solutions are highly ineffective and will be unable to sustain our population in the near future.

Read More

The Solution

Using somatic cells, specifically skin fibroblasts we are able to turn the cells into Induced Pluripotent Stem Cells, and then to healthy germ cells: eggs and sperm. Following this, using a tissue engineered environment the egg is matured, and in vitro fertilization occurs prior to implanting the embryo within the uterus.

Read More

The Outcome

Through this technique we are able to provide every couple with the an equal opportunity to reproduce, and have the dream of a family. Currently, over 186 million individuals worldwide struggle with infertility, and at Janam Labs we are able to provide every couple including same-sex couples a chance of family.
